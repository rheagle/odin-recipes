PB Cookies

Description

Soft, chewy, and bursting with peanut butter flavor, these classic cookies are a timeless treat. Perfect with a glass of milk or as a midday snack, theyâ€™re simple to make and impossible to resist!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Kraft Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Mix all ingredients until blended.
  3. Roll into 24 (1 inch) balls. Place 2 inches apart on baking sheet. Flatten each, in criss-cross pattern, with tines of fork.
  4. Bake 15 minutes or until lightly browned (do not overbake). Cool on baking sheets 5 minutes. Remove to wire racks; Cool completely.
